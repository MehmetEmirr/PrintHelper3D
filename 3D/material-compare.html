Which Material Should You Choose?

For selling on Etsy or similar platforms, PLA is usually the starting point — lowest cost, easiest to print, widest color selection.

If you're printing functional parts (brackets, clips, housings), PETG offers a great balance of cost and durability. It's only ~30–50% more expensive than PLA but significantly tougher.

For miniatures, jewelry, or premium display items, Resin allows you to charge significantly more — market rates for high-detail resin prints are often 3–4× FDM prices.

Nylon is the most expensive but allows you to target the engineering and industrial segment, where customers care about specs more than price.
