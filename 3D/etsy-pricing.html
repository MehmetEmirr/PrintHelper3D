Fees, margins & what actually sells

Etsy is one of the best platforms for selling 3D printed items — but its fee structure can eat into your margins if you don't account for them correctly. Here's exactly what you need to know.

Total Effective Fee

On a $20 item sold in the US: Etsy takes roughly $2.30–$2.65 before you see a cent. Always add this to your cost calculation.

Etsy Pricing Tips

Include shipping in price: Free shipping listings rank higher on Etsy. Bake the average shipping cost into your item price.

Price in round numbers: $24.99 or $25 outperforms $22.38. Round up to the nearest psychologically appealing price.

Offer variations: Color/size options increase average order value. Each variation needs its own margin check.

Track your bestsellers: Once you find a winner, optimize the print (faster speeds, less infill) to improve margin without changing the price.
